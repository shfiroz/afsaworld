Our Story

We started as a small street cart with one simple mission: serve the freshest tea and warmest maska buns to our community. Every cup is brewed fresh to order, and our buns are baked daily.

What makes us special? We use best tea, fresh milk, and butter for our maska buns. Each flavor is crafted with care and best ingredients.
